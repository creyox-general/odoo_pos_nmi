NMI Payment Terminal


Overview


The NMI POS Payment Terminal Integration with Odoo is a seamless solution designed to enhance the payment experience in the Odoo POS system. With a simple click of the "NMI" payment method, the system sends the payment request to the Nmi terminal. Once the user taps or swipes their card, the transaction is automatically processed, and the order is completed in Odoo POS without any manual input. This integration ensures faster transactions, minimizes errors, and improves the overall efficiency of your POS system, offering a smooth and secure payment process for both merchants and customers.
